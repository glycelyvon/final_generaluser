Five easy steps to start on CompaniON

CompaniON is a platform designed to help individuals find companions for various activities, whether you need a friend for a casual event or simply someone to hang out with. Here’s how it works!

Register and Log In

Create an account and sign in.

Find Your Companion

Browse and select a companion.

Check their availability

See if they’re available for your chosen time.

Choose a Place

Pick the location for your meetup.

Finalize Your Booking!

Confirm and complete the booking process.
