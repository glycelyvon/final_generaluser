Milo Marathon

The Milo Marathon is the Philippines' premier running event, bringing together athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and families for a day of fun, fitness, and community. With a legacy spanning over four decades, the Milo Marathon is more than just a race—it's a celebration of perseverance, sportsmanship, and active living.

The Milo Marathon welcomes participants of all levels—from elite athletes chasing personal bests to casual joggers and first-timers. With categories like the 42K marathon, 21K half, 10K, 5K fun run, and 3K family run, there’s something for everyone. Beyond the race, it fosters values of discipline, determination, and teamwork. Part of the proceeds supports sports equipment donations to underprivileged schools, inspiring future generations to live actively.

BarakoFest 2024

The Barako Fest is a vibrant celebration of Lipa City's cultural pride and heritage, centered around its world-renowned Barako coffee. Held annually, this event brings together locals and visitors to experience the rich flavors, traditions, and artistry that make Batangas coffee unique.

njoy a variety of activities, including coffee tastings, brewing competitions, cultural performances, and local artisan markets. With food stalls offering Batangueño delicacies and live entertainment, the festival is a perfect blend of tradition and festivity. Come to Lipa's Barako Fest, sip the bold brew that defines a region, and celebrate the culture and spirit of Batangas!

CICS Day

The CICS Day at Batangas State University Lipa is a dynamic celebration that showcases the talents and ingenuity of students from the College of Informatics and Computing Sciences (CICS). The event features a variety of exciting activities, including coding hackathons, tech talks and seminars, e-sports tournaments, IT exhibits, and creative competitions, promoting both academic excellence and student camaraderie.

This event highlights innovation, teamwork, and leadership while providing opportunities for students to connect, learn, and grow. Whether you're passionate about technology or just looking to have fun, CICS Week offers something for everyone. Come join CICS Week at BatStateU Lipa and celebrate technology, teamwork, and innovation—one step closer to shaping the future!

The Outlets at Lipa

The Outlets at Lipa is a premier shopping and lifestyle destination located in the heart of Batangas. Known for its open-air layout and year-round discounts, it offers a variety of local and international brands at outlet prices. From fashion, footwear, and sportswear to home essentials and electronics, there’s something for everyone.

Beyond shopping, The Outlets at Lipa features dining options, recreational spaces, and events areas, making it a perfect venue for family outings and gatherings. Whether you're looking for great deals, delicious meals, or a day of leisure, The Outlets at Lipa promises a refreshing retail experience with something for every lifestyle.

SM City Lipa

SM City Lipa is the region’s go-to hub for shopping, dining, and entertainment. With its wide array of fashion boutiques, restaurants, supermarkets, and recreational spaces, it caters to the diverse needs of individuals and families. Known for hosting seasonal events, sales, and activities, SM Lipa offers more than just shopping—it’s a lifestyle destination.

From cinemas and arcades to cafés and food courts, SM Lipa ensures that every visit is a fun-filled experience. Whether you're running errands, enjoying a meal, or catching a movie, SM City Lipa has it all under one roof!
